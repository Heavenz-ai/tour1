#Communnity

Community Support; Giving Back To The Covmmunity

Donate
footonkilitanzania Adventure is committed to responsible tourism, sustainability of the natural environment and well being of the people of Africa.

In our effort to operate an Eco-friendly business, we have partnered with several local schools and conservation organization in Tanzania to help promote conservation, environment awareness, and community involvement in a way that is pleasurable and enlightening. Our donations provide scholarships, books, and supplies to local children with their daily needs such as food and housing as well as financial aid to cover school fees and other educational needs. We consider in giving back to the communities that share their motherland with us. When you travel with us, not only are you choosing an Eco-friendly holiday, but you are also making a ccontribution to the sustainable development of this country.

Our Foot On Kili Tanzania Community trips we visit:
Local schools in remote areas
Hospitals in remote villages
Participation on Human development projects
Local markets to buy local products from the farmers families
Street children centers.
We share life experience with the community and donate directly to them.
Please contact Foot On Kili Tanzania Adventure.
